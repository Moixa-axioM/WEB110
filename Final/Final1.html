The Mass Effect Universe!

What is Mass Effect?

Mass Effect is a Sci-fi RPG video game released in 2008 by Bioware. Mass Effect was the start of an expansive universe which now spans into 4 mainline games, 2 spinoff games, books, tv shows, movies and hopefully many more in the future!

The Setting Explained

The Mass Effect stories are set in the 22nd century. After discovering the first Mass Relay, located near Pluto, humanity would expand throughout the Milkyway galaxy . With this newly discovered technology, humanity joined and explored many other races and cultures in this vast universe. All congregated together at a single point, The Citadel, these races consider galactic politics on a larger scale. The Citadel and its current borders in the Milkyway are led by the council. The council consists of three - four major races. The Asari, The Turians, The Salarians, and then of course Humanity.

The Mainline Story

The Main events of the Mass Effect Universe are centered around a mysterious and vicious old mechanical race nicknamed "The Reapers". After every 50,000 years, these Machines come from "Dark Space" outside our galaxy to devour any civilization that has been deemed intelligent enough to create their own AI life. You play as Commander Shepard throughout the Main 3 games of this Trilogy in a fight to save the galaxy from these Reapers by making hard decisions that will effect the outcome of your game. for more information about each game please visit the Games section of this website!

Key Factions throughout the Universe

The Citadel Council
As explained earlier, This is the central government for a vast majority of the Milky-way. All the races provide support for this central government The have a Joint Military, but their greatest asset are the Spectres, who are essentially secret agents who are above the law and will do whatever is neccesary to achieve their objectives.
The Asari
These long-lived, blue skinned, all female race are well known for their Biotics (psychic abilities) and are the first race to discover the Mass Relays and the Citadel. They are a key member of the Council.
The Turians
A hyper militaristic bird-like species, turians are known for their sense of duty and order. They strictly obey their hierarchy and have the largest Military of all the species in the Milkyway
The Salarians
A lizard-like race who are known for their hyper-intellect and espionage abilities. They are the shortest lived race due to their fast metabolisms living only to be around 40.
The Alliance
The main faction for Humanity, The Alliance stands for humanity as their main form of government based back on Earth. They boast a large Military and are closely based off of the current U.S. Navy.
The Quarians
This nomadic species are known for their advanced technology. They are unfortunately stuck inside suits for most of their life due to their weakened immune system. As such, most do not know exactly what the Quarians look like underneath. They are also the creators of the Geth
The Geth
A syntheic race, the Geth were created by the Quarians as slave robots to do work for them. Through a hive-mind like intelligence, the Geth evolved into a full-fledged AI race and rebelled against the Quarians, eventually ejecting them from their own homeworld.
The Krogan
A reptilian race known for their brute strength and durability. After rebelling against the council many years ago, they were sterilized by the Genophage. Now their society has collapsed and only 1 in 1000 children survive childbirth. Most now work as mercenaries or pirates.
Cerberus
A once Alliance black-ops group is now a Human-supremecist paramilitary group known for their unethical experiments across the Galaxy. Led by a mysterious man named 'The Illusive Man'
