Mass Effect Mainline Games

Mass Effect

The first game in the main trilogy, Mass Effect takes place in the year 2183. You play as Commander Shepard, an Alliance soldier who is under consideration to be come a Council Spectre. After and attack on a Human Colony, Shepard sees a vision from a Prothean beacon. Although unsure what this beacon's vision is about, Shepard must take his/her crew on a space adventure spanning many planets to stop a rogue Spectre named Saren. Much is revealed about the beacon's vision and its connection to Saren, Shepard fights through the rogue AI geth and pirates alike to take down Saren's plan. ***SPOILERS FROM THIS PART ON*** After the vision is revealed to be about the Reapers, Shepard finds that Saren is plotting to bring the Reapers to our Galaxy to end all civilized life as we know it. Through sheer heroics and relying on his/her allies, Shepard then takes the fight to Saren and eventually stops the invasion.

Mass Effect 2

Months after the events of Mass Effect, Shepard is sent on his/her ship to take out any pockets of the Geth that remain. During one of these excursions Shepard is attacked by a Large alien ship and his/her ship, the Normandy is destroyed. Shepard dies to save their crew and crashes down to the planet below. Two years later, Shepard wakes up after being Reconstructed by the mysterious group called Cerberus. After being ressurected, Shepard is then tasked with finding out why Human Colonies are going missing. The culprit is revealed to be the Collectors who are working for the Reapers Reluctantly Shepard works with the Illusive Man and Cerberus to field and team and save the human colonies by going through the Omega-4 Relay and taking the fight to the collectors base. Through the players own decisions, Shepard and his/her team are sent on this suicide mission and may or may not make it back alive, but eventually stop the collectors in their tracks.

Mass Effect 3

The Finale of the main Trilogy, Mass Effect 3 starts with Shepard on Earth as the Reaper invasion finally begins. The Reapers are finally here and launch a devastating invasion across the entire galaxy. The last chance for Humanity and life across the galaxy is for Commander Shepard to leave Earth and unite as many races as possible. From resolving age old rivalries to finishing ongoing wars, Shepard along with their crew must create a united task force to take back humanities homeworld: Earth. Using an ancient superweapon found on the archive on mars, Shepard along with much of the galaxy's resistance launch their final attack on Earth which as become the central hub for the Reaper invasion. Using the Crucible Shepard must make a hard decision on how to resolve the invasion... How will it end? That depends on you!

Mass Effect: Andromeda

In the year 2185, before the Reaper invasion, the Andromeda Initiative was created to send life to another galaxy. 4 Arks were went across space to the Andromeda Galaxy and 600 years later, they finally arrive. Instead of being shepard in this game, you are instead known as the Pathfinder, Ryder. Ryder is tasked with making sure humanity, and the other races can inhabit this distant galaxy. They face many challenges such as the hostile Kett,the dangerous cloud of dark energy known as the scourge, and the unknown Remnants. Tasked with humanities survival, Ryder's journey is decided by the players decisions. How will Humanity survive in this departure from the mainline series?
